Our flagship IPA! Hazy and absolutely packed with flavour, the trifecta of US Ekuanot, Amarillo, and Ahtanum hops produce a multi-tone tropical fiesta of hop flavour and aroma including pineapple, orange, and lemon. A welcoming bitter edge rounds out this offering which represents the cutting edge of our brewing capability.
